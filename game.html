Beautiful Dream

Download for Market Download for Windows Download for macOS

Creative Vision

This is an extremely personal narrative work, full of details and fragments from my memory. Perhaps in the eyes of others, it will appear a little hazy and difficult to describe, but in any case, I hope it will bring you a delicate feeling.

Just as I always insist: find your own way and direction.

Game Description

When you open your eyes, you find yourself back in 2015 - familiar classroom, young classmates, everything seems to have never changed. But are you traveling from the reality ten years later, or are all your current experiences just a dream about the future?

In this short and gentle time travel, you will make your own choice between memories and reality.

Escape, escape the room

Download Escape, escape the room

Creative Vision

As a suspense film lover, escape rooms have always been my favorite theme. This is also a personal narrative. Whether it is a cat, a teacup or a note, there are some ideas. For example, is warm tea poisonous? What names are recorded in the note full of names? There are many things I want to expand, but the content is limited, so it may seem simple.

Game Description

You find yourself trapped in a strange room. Other creatures will lead you out, but mysterious words and centuries-old trees hint at a time and space disorder. So, have you found your way? Have you gotten out?
